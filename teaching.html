During the 2025–2026 academic year, I am a visiting professor in the Department of Government at Harvard University. Below are my courses for Spring 2026.

Spring 2026 — Harvard University

Gov 2001: Quantitative Social Science Methods I

Level: PhD (first-year methods sequence)

Meeting: Monday & Wednesday, 1:30–2:45 PM

Description: A rigorous introduction to probability theory, statistical inference, and linear regression for first-year PhD students. We take a "population-first" approach: define what you want to know about the population before worrying about estimation.
