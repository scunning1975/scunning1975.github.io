About Me

I am a professor of economics at Baylor University. I study a number of topics including mental healthcare, sex work, abortion and drug policy. I love my family, friends, colleagues, students, HBO, Always Sunny in Philadelphia, causal inference, Mac Miller and Kanye West. Check out my free online textbook, Causal Inference: the Mixtape (with accompanying Spotify playlist). Yale University Press is publishing an update to the original pdf and is available on preorder (and will be avilable for real on January 26th, 2021) now. The new physical book expands the chapters to make the book more contemporary, including examples in R as well as Stata, and so you should totally get it. The book can be purchased from such vendors as Amazon and Yale University Press for a mere $35! That's like buying an unusually expensive cup of coffee!

I will also post a free online HTML version of the Mixtape to my website hopefully around January 3rd, 2021. The website will include interactive examples from R in addition to being beautiful to gaze upon.
