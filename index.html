About Me

I am a professor of economics at Baylor University. I study a number of topics at the intersection of crime and risky behavior. I am particularly interested in the effects of technology and policy on sex work. I love my family, friends, and students, HBO TV, causal inference and Kanye West. I wrote this "Getting into an Economics PhD” for my students in Uruguay. Check out my free online textbook, “Causal Inference: the Mixtape" (with accompanying Spotify playlist). The book will be published on January 26th, 2021 by Yale University Press and is a substantial updating to the free version that is available above. You can download the book at Amazon for only $35!
