Mixtape Sessions

Periodically throughout the year, I and other instructors will host online classes on causal inference. You can find a link to them . These classes will provide in-depth training in causal inference methodologies such as regression discontinuity, difference-in-differences, instrumental variables. They are priced using a price discrimination and bundling model. Their price is $595/person for my causal inference workshop, and $995/person for both causal inference and my advanced difference-in-differences workshop. But if you are an enrolled student, predoc, postdoc or someone from a low or middle income country, the price is $50/person or $90/person for the two workshops. Please check out the website, look around, and see what you think.

\textbf{In person workshops}: But, for those who are interested in having me come visit your department, firm or agency to do a multi-day workshop on causal inference methodology, that too can be worked out. Please email Emmi Scott at emmi.scott@mixtape.consulting to arrange this.
